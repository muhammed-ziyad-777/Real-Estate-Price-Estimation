About This Real Estate Price Prediction Project

This project predicts house prices in Bengaluru using machine learning models trained on real-world data. The model considers multiple factors such as location, area type, number of bedrooms, bathrooms, balconies, and total square footage to estimate house prices accurately.

Besides price prediction, the project includes features like comparing average prices between two different locations and visualizing pricing trends.

The goal is to help buyers and sellers make informed decisions by providing reliable price estimates and insights into the real estate market.
